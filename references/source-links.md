# Official Source Links and Provenance

This public skill stores rule summaries and executable profiles, but does not redistribute third-party guideline PDFs, article PDFs, images, or Illustrator templates. Before final submission, refresh these links and verify the current official instructions.

## Nature Main

Official/public source pages used to derive the Nature profile:

- Nature Portfolio artwork and figure preparation guidance: https://www.nature.com/nature-portfolio/for-authors/write#how-to-submit
- Nature Research Journals guide to preparing final artwork PDF, if still publicly available from Nature: https://www.nature.com/documents/NRJs-guide-to-preparing-final-artwork.pdf
- Nature Portfolio permissions/reuse information: https://www.nature.com/nature-portfolio/reprints-and-permissions/permissions-requests

Profile rules retained in this skill:

- Single-column width: `89 mm`.
- Double-column width: `183 mm`.
- Optional intermediate width: `120-136 mm`, only when justified.
- Arial-first sans-serif typography, with Helvetica-compatible fallbacks.
- Conservative label target: `7 pt`; warn below `5 pt`.
- RGB color workflow for original research figures.
- Raster elements at final size, at least `300 dpi`.
- Prefer editable vector output for charts and line art.

## Science Main

Official/public source pages used to derive the Science profile:

- Science journals information for authors: https://www.science.org/content/page/science-information-authors
- Science journals editorial policies and author resources: https://www.science.org/content/page/science-journals-editorial-policies
- Science/AAAS terms of service: https://www.science.org/content/page/terms-service

Profile rules retained in this skill:

- Single-column width: `9 cm / 3.6 in`.
- Double-column width: `18.3 cm / 7.25 in`.
- Arial or Helvetica-compatible typography.
- Panel labels: `10 pt` bold, uppercase.
- Figure labels: `6-9 pt`.
- Minimum final printed line width: `0.28 pt`.
- Photographic raster images at final size: `300-500 dpi`.
- Embedded images in vector files: at least `300 ppi`.
- Full-color artwork in RGB.
- Avoid red/green-only encoding; add redundant shape, texture, marker, or line-style encoding when needed.

## Public Distribution Note

Do not commit downloaded third-party PDFs, article figures, Illustrator templates, or publisher-provided assets unless you have explicit redistribution permission. Keep local copies outside the public repository when they are needed for audit or private use.
